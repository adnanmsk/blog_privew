Create an architecture diagram for a complex Computer Vision pipeline called the "Bat Swing Plane Pipeline". The pipeline takes raw mobile phone video of a cricket batsman and generates a fully stabilized, 3D animated trajectory of their bat swing.

Please structure the diagram using the following groups and connections:

1. **Group: Pre-Processing**
   * **Raw Video Input**: The starting point
   * **Action Trimming (YOLOv8)**: Detects the batsman and trims the video down to a 1-second action clip.
   * **Pitch Stabilization **: Removes severe camera shake by tracking ground features.
   * *Connections*: Raw Video Input -> Action Trimming -> Pitch Stabilization.

2. **Group: AI Inference (Parallel Branching)**
   * **Depth Mapping (DepthAnything V2)**: Generates dense relative Z-axis depth maps for every frame.
   * **Zero-Shot Segmentation (SAM 2)**: Uses a dual-session subtraction trick to generate clean masks of the cricket bat (ignoring the wickets).
   * *Connections*: Pitch Stabilization connects to BOTH Depth Mapping and Zero-Shot Segmentation.

3. **Group: Physics & 3D Fusion**
   * **PCA Sub-Pixel Geometry**: Analyzes the 2D Bat Masks to extract the exact X,Y pixel coordinates of the bat's handle and tip.
   * **3D Kalman Filter Smoothing**: Fuses the X,Y coordinates from the PCA step with the Z-Depth coordinates from the Depth Maps to create mathematically smooth, gap-filled 3D points in space.
   * *Connections*: Zero-Shot Segmentation -> PCA Sub-Pixel Geometry -> 3D Kalman Filter Smoothing. 
   * *Connections*: Depth Mapping -> 3D Kalman Filter Smoothing.

4. **Group: Final Output**
   * **3D Trajectory Renderer**: Uses Matplotlib and FFmpeg to output a colored 3D ribbon animation of the bat swing.
   * *Connections*: 3D Kalman Filter Smoothing -> 3D Trajectory Renderer.

Please make the AI Inference group visually distinct (perhaps side-by-side nodes), use icons for the nodes (like a camera for video, a brain for AI, a math symbol for PCA), and ensure the layout flows cleanly from top to bottom or left to right.
